Our story

Lumina Home is a small catalog of considered objects, designed in Copenhagen and made by independent studios across Northern Europe.

Founded in 2018

By a husband-and-wife team — a former cabinet-maker and a former glass blower — who wanted to sell the kind of objects they'd grown up with.

Twelve makers

We work with twelve independent studios. Each one is small enough that the people who own the studio are the people who make the work.

One shipment a season

We restock four times a year, in spring, summer, autumn, and winter. We don't carry seasonal trends. We don't run sales.

How we choose what to carry

A short list of principles that's served us well.

Made by hand, mostly

A machine can finish a piece, but a person should design it and shape it. We pass on anything that's been engineered to look handmade rather than be handmade.

Repair before replace

If a Lumina piece breaks, send it back. We repair, refinish, and return — no questions, no fee for the first ten years.

Honest pricing

Our markup is the same on every piece. When the price changes, it's because the maker's cost changed, never because we ran a campaign.
