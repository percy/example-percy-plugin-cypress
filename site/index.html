Made to last

Three principles that guide every piece in our catalog.

Independent makers

Every piece is made by a studio of fewer than twelve people. We visit each workshop in person, know the makers by name, and print their story on the label — so you always know exactly whose hands built the object standing in your home.

Natural materials

Solid wood, hand-thrown clay, untreated linen — no plastics, no veneers, no shortcuts. Every material is chosen to age gracefully and be repaired rather than replaced, and we publish the full sourcing for each object so that nothing about how it was made stays hidden.

Built to repair

If a piece ever breaks, send it back to us. We mend, refinish, and return it — usually within two weeks and often at no charge at all. A considered object should last for decades rather than a single season, and we stand behind that promise for the life of the piece.
