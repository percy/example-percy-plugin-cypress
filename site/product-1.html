Arc Table Lamp

$189

A small lamp with a long shadow. The Arc was designed by Studio Hjem in 2021 as a desk companion — quiet enough to live in a bedroom, warm enough to read by.

Solid brass arm, hand-blown opal glass shade, in-line dimmer on a textile cord. Bulb included.

Details

Materials

Brushed brass arm, opal glass shade, woven cotton cord. Lead-free finish.

Dimensions

Height 42cm, reach 38cm, base diameter 14cm. Cord length 1.8m.

Bulb

E27 / E26, 6W LED included. Warm white, 2700K, dimmable.
